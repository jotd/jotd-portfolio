Origin Story

I'm Jot Dhaliwal, a Product Designer at Winnie and Adjunct Instructor at Tradecraft. Previously I worked at Snap, Twitter and earned a B.A. from Berkeley.

My passion for design began as a kid collecting Retro Jordans, reading GQ and drooling over Ralph Lauren displays at the mall. Creative influences include album covers, fine art and fashion photography.

Prototyping with code is my favorite part of the design process because I can bring static mockups to life. I strive to work on products made with craft ✌️
