JSD Creative Inc. is a product design & motion studio founded by Jot & Jess Dhaliwal in 2022. The two siblings developed a passion for craft, theatre & storytelling in a Punjabi Sikh household filled with rich culture, tradition and honor.
After graduating from Berkeley and USC respectively, Jot & Jess developed expertise in the tech, media, art and luxury sectors. Now Jot & Jess partner with top tier founders on the frontier of crypto, web3 & entertainment to elevate the design & innovation of their businesses.
We offer product design through a monthly subscription. Typically we come in after a founder has successfuly secured Series A funding — 4 month minimum with 1st month as a 30 day trial run to see if the vibe is right ✌️
